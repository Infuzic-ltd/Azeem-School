Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Requirements

Minimum Education Level

Applicants must have completed secondary education (O-levels / Matric or equivalent) or hold a prior diploma/degree for advanced programs.

English Proficiency

A basic level of English is required. IELTS, TOEFL, or equivalent certificates are accepted. Non-native speakers may take our placement test.

Valid Identification

A government-issued ID (CNIC, passport, or birth certificate for minors) is required for identity verification during enrollment.

Academic Transcripts

Official transcripts or mark sheets from your last attended institution are required. Certified copies are acceptable at the application stage.

No Age Restriction

We welcome students of all ages. Whether you are a fresh graduate or a working professional, there is a program tailored for you.

Spring Intake — February

Applications open in December. Early applicants receive priority enrollment and scholarship consideration.

Fall Intake — September

Our largest intake of the year. Applications open in June. Join thousands of new students each autumn.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit-based scholarships cover up to 50% of tuition. Need-based grants and flexible instalment plans are also available.
