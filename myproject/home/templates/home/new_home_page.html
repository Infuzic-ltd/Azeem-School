About Us

Cultivating Knowledge, Character & Excellence

For over 25 years, Azeem School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Karachi — blending rigorous academics with Islamic values and modern teaching methods.

  • Affiliated with Federal Board (FBISE) and Sindh Board (BSEK).

  • Cambridge CAIE O & A Level programmes available.

  • Over 1,500 students from Pre-Montessori to Grade 12.
