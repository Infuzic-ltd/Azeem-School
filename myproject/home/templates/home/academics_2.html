Assessments

How We Evaluate Progress

Our multi-layered assessment system ensures every student's growth is measured fairly, comprehensively, and continuously throughout the year.

Continuous Assessment

Daily classwork, homework, and class participation keep students engaged and provide teachers with real-time progress insights.

20% Weightage

Unit Tests

Conducted after each topic to assess comprehension and enable timely remediation before cumulative examinations.

20% Weightage

Mid-Term Exams

Structured examinations at the midpoint of each academic term, covering all topics taught in the first half.

25% Weightage

Annual Examinations

Comprehensive end-of-year exams covering the full syllabus, determining promotion and board examination eligibility.

35% Weightage
