Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at info@azeemschool.edu.pk

Students require previous academic records, a school leaving certificate, and must pass our entry assessment. Admissions are open for Classes I through XII. Please visit our Admissions page for complete details and required documents.
Campus visits can be arranged by calling our admissions office at +92 300 123 4567 or emailing admissions@azeemschool.edu.pk. Visits are available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 2 PM, and Saturdays by prior appointment.
Azeem School follows Lahore Board (BISE Lahore), Federal Board (FBISE), and Cambridge (O/A Levels). Students and parents may select the preferred board at the time of enrollment subject to availability.
Yes, school transport is available from major residential areas including DHA, Gulberg, Model Town, Johar Town, and Bahria Town. Routes and timings are shared upon enrollment. Contact our transport coordinator for availability in your area.
Fee structures vary by class level and chosen board. Monthly, quarterly, and annual payment plans are available. Sibling discounts and need-based scholarships are also offered. Please contact our accounts office for a detailed fee schedule.
