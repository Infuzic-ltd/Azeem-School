Hands-On Learning

State-of-the-Art Laboratories

Purpose-built labs that turn textbook theory into real-world discovery for every grade level.

Physics Lab

Equipped with precision instruments for mechanics, optics, electricity, and modern physics experiments.

  • Oscilloscopes & signal generators
  • Optical bench & laser kits
  • Digital measurement tools
  • Capacity: 30 students

Chemistry Lab

Fully ventilated lab with fume hoods, reagent storage, and safety gear for practical experiments.

  • Fume extraction hoods
  • Titration & distillation sets
  • Digital weighing balances
  • Capacity: 28 students

Biology Lab

Microscopy, dissection, and ecology experiments in a safe, specimen-rich environment.

  • Compound & stereo microscopes
  • Specimen & model collections
  • Genetics experiment kits
  • Capacity: 30 students

Computer Lab

Core-i5 workstations, dual-monitor setups, and industry-standard software for coding and design.

  • 40 high-performance PCs
  • 500 Mbps fibre internet
  • Adobe CC & Microsoft 365
  • Capacity: 40 students

Robotics & STEM Lab

LEGO Mindstorms, Arduino, and 3D-printing stations for budding engineers and innovators.

  • 3D printers & laser cutter
  • Arduino & Raspberry Pi kits
  • VR exploration headsets
  • Capacity: 24 students

Language Lab

Digital audio-visual stations for immersive English, Urdu, and foreign language practice.

  • Individual headphone booths
  • Speech recognition software
  • Interactive listening modules
  • Capacity: 35 students

Biometric Entry Control

CCTV Surveillance & Access Control

Over 120 cameras cover all entry points, corridors, playgrounds, and parking areas. Biometric access restricts unauthorised entry to any zone.

Medical Room & First Aid

Two qualified nurses are present throughout school hours. The medical room is stocked with essential medicines, an AED defibrillator, and emergency response kits.

Fire Safety & Emergency Drills

Fire suppression systems, clearly marked exits, and mandatory bi-annual evacuation drills ensure full campus readiness for emergencies.

Safe School Transport

GPS-tracked school buses with trained drivers and female attendants cover all major routes. Real-time tracking is available to parents through our portal.

Hygienic Cafeteria

Supervised canteen with government-approved food standards. A nutritionist-approved menu includes hot meals, healthy snacks, and safe drinking water stations.

Prayer Room & Counselling Centre

Separate prayer halls for boys and girls, plus a dedicated counselling centre staffed by a qualified school psychologist for student wellbeing.
